<p><p>Advanced composite materials in construction are categorized into four main types. Polymer Matrix Composites (PMC) utilize polymers as a matrix, offering lightweight and corrosion-resistant properties, ideal for various applications. Metal Matrix Composites (MMC) combine metals with ceramic reinforcements, delivering enhanced strength and thermal stability for structural components. Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC) exhibit high-temperature resistance and are used in harsh environments. Other market segments may include hybrid composites and bio-based materials, expanding the versatility and applications in construction.</p></p>

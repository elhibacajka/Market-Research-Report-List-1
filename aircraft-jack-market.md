<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft jack market comprises primarily hydraulic and pneumatic types, each serving distinct needs in aviation maintenance. Hydraulic jacks use fluid pressure for lifting heavy aircraft, offering stability and high lifting capacity, ideal for large planes. Pneumatic jacks, on the other hand, rely on compressed air, making them lighter and faster for smaller aircraft or tasks requiring quick elevation and repositioning. Both types enhance efficiency and safety in aircraft servicing, contributing to streamlined operations in the aerospace industry.</p></p>

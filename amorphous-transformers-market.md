<p><p>The amorphous transformers market is divided into two main types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed transformers use mineral or synthetic oils for cooling and insulation, offering efficient heat dissipation and enhanced performance, especially in high-load applications. Dry-type transformers, on the other hand, use air as a cooling medium and are designed for environments where fire safety and maintenance are critical. Both types leverage amorphous steel technology to reduce energy losses and improve efficiency, making them suitable for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>

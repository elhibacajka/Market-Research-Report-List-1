<p><p>The automation pipette tips market is segmented into non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for standard liquid handling tasks and are typically used for non-volatile liquids, making them cost-effective and efficient for high-throughput applications. In contrast, filtered pipette tips incorporate a barrier to prevent contamination and liquid carryover, making them ideal for sensitive samples and PCR applications where accuracy and sterility are paramount. Both types cater to diverse laboratory needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The clinical mass spectrometer market is categorized into three main types: LC-MS, GC-MS, and others. LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) is widely used for analyzing complex biological samples, offering high sensitivity and specificity. GC-MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) is primarily employed for volatile compounds and is effective in analyzing environmental and food samples. The "others" segment includes hybrid systems and specialized techniques, catering to specific applications in pharmaceuticals, toxicology, and proteomics, broadening the scope of analytical capabilities in clinical settings.</p></p>

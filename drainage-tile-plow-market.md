<p><p>The drainage tile plow market primarily features two types: pull-type and mounted-type plows. Pull-type plows are towed by a tractor, offering flexibility and ease of transport over varied terrains, making them suitable for larger fields. In contrast, mounted-type plows are directly attached to the tractor, providing better maneuverability in tight spaces and enhancing precision during operation. Both types are designed to facilitate efficient installation of drainage tiles, improving agricultural productivity by managing soil moisture levels effectively.</p></p>

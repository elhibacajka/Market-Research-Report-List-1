<p><p>Enteral and parenteral nutrition devices and consumables facilitate nutritional delivery. Enteral devices include nasogastric tubes and orogastric tubes for direct intestine access, and entero-oral tubes connecting to oral intake. Nutrient pumps regulate enteral feedings. Parenteral devices comprise infusion pumps for intravenous nutrient delivery and intravenous catheters for direct blood access. These devices cater to patients unable to consume food orally, supporting critical nutrition needs in hospitals and home care settings while ensuring effective and safe nutrient administration.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Glucose Excipient Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anhydrous Glucose</li><li>Glucose Monohydrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glucose excipient market includes two primary types: anhydrous glucose and glucose monohydrate. Anhydrous glucose is a dry form of glucose, often used for its high solubility and stability in pharmaceutical formulations. Glucose monohydrate, on the other hand, contains one molecule of water, making it easier to handle in certain applications and providing a stable source of glucose with controlled moisture content. Both types serve essential roles as sweeteners, stabilizers, and energy sources in various medicinal and nutritional products.</p></p>

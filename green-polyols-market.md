<p><p>The Green Polyols market is primarily divided into two categories: Polyether Polyols and Polyester Polyols. Polyether Polyols are derived from renewable resources and are known for their flexibility and resistance to hydrolysis, making them suitable for applications like flexible foams and coatings. Conversely, Polyester Polyols, also sourced from renewables, offer higher thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for rigid foams and elastomers. Both types contribute to sustainable practices in industries such as furniture, automotive, and construction.</p></p>

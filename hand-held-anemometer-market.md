<p><p>The hand-held anemometer market includes several types, each serving specific purposes. Cup anemometers measure wind speed using rotating cups, while vane anemometers utilize blades to gauge airflow direction and velocity. Hot-wire anemometers detect temperature changes in a heated wire to assess air speed. Laser Doppler anemometers employ laser beams for precise airflow measurement, while ultrasonic anemometers use sound waves to determine wind speed and direction. Additionally, various other specialized anemometers cater to niche applications within the market.</p></p>

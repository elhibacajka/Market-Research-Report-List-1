<p><p>The hydraulic equipment market can be categorized into three main types: pumps and motors, cylinders, and valves. Pumps and motors are essential for converting mechanical energy into hydraulic energy, driving fluid flow in systems. Cylinders use pressurized fluid to create linear motion and perform work. Valves control the flow and direction of the hydraulic fluid, ensuring the system operates efficiently. Collectively, these components form the backbone of hydraulic systems used across various industries, from manufacturing to construction.</p></p>

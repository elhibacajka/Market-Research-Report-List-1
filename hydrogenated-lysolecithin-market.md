<p><p>The hydrogenated lysolecithin market is categorized into two main types: fluid hydrolyzed and deoiled hydrolyzed. Fluid hydrolyzed lysolecithin retains its moisture content, offering enhanced emulsification properties and stability in various formulations, making it ideal for food and cosmetic applications. Deoiled hydrolyzed lysolecithin, on the other hand, has had its oil removed, resulting in a more concentrated form that is often used in dietary supplements and pharmaceutical products for its purity and functionality. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>

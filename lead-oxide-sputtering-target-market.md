<p><p>The lead oxide sputtering target market is divided into two main types: rotatable transformation and non-rotating type. Rotatable transformation targets allow continuous material deposition as they can rotate during the sputtering process, enhancing uniformity and efficiency. Non-rotating type targets, in contrast, remain stationary, which may lead to localized depletion of the target material but can be simpler in design and cost-effective for specific applications. Each type caters to different production needs and technical requirements in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The Low Rolling Resistance Truck and Bus Radial Tire market consists of two primary types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias Tires feature layers of fabric crisscrossing at various angles, providing strong durability but typically higher rolling resistance. In contrast, Radial Tires have steel belts running perpendicular to the tread, enhancing fuel efficiency and offering improved performance, handling, and longevity due to reduced rolling resistance. Both types serve distinct needs within the commercial transportation sector, impacting operational costs and environmental footprints.</p></p>

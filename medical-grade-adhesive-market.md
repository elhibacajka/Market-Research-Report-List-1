<p><p>The medical grade adhesive market is categorized into two primary types: natural and synthetic. Synthetic adhesives, on the other hand, are chemically engineered for enhanced strength and durability, often used in surgical procedures and medical device assembly. Both types cater to specific applications in the healthcare industry, addressing varying needs for adhesion, healing, and patient safety.</p></p>

<p><p>The medical low-temperature sterilization equipment market comprises several types, including Ethylene Oxide (ETO) Sterilization, Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilization, and Plasma Sterilization. ETO sterilization is widely used for heat-sensitive devices, employing ethylene oxide gas to eradicate microorganisms. Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilization utilizes vaporized hydrogen peroxide to achieve effective sterilization at low temperatures. Plasma Sterilization is a newer technology that uses ionized gas to sterilize items quickly and safely. Other methods may include ozone and formaldehyde sterilization, catering to specific medical needs.</p></p>

<p><p>Medical treatment tables are categorized based on their backrest functionalities. Flat top tables provide a stable surface without adjustable features, ideal for basic procedures. Manual backrest tables allow healthcare providers to adjust the angle of the backrest manually, offering flexibility during treatments. Pneumatic backrest tables utilize air pressure mechanisms for smooth adjustments, enhancing patient comfort. Power backrest tables feature electric controls for effortless positioning, enabling precise adjustments and improving workflow efficiency in clinical settings.</p></p>

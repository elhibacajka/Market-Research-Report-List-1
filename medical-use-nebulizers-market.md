<p><p>The medical nebulizers market includes three main types: pneumatic nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to aerosolize medication, making them effective for various respiratory conditions. Ultrasonic nebulizers use ultrasonic vibrations to create mist, offering faster medication delivery and quieter operation. Mesh nebulizers employ a mesh membrane to produce aerosols, resulting in efficient medication delivery and portability. Each type has unique advantages suited for different patient needs and treatment settings.</p></p>

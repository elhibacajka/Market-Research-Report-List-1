<p><ul><li>Independent GPU</li><li>Integrated GPU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile GPU market is primarily divided into two categories: independent GPUs and integrated GPUs. Independent GPUs are dedicated graphics processors that offer superior performance for gaming and graphic-intensive applications, operating independently of the CPU. In contrast, integrated GPUs are built into the CPU, sharing resources and power, making them more energy-efficient for everyday tasks. While independent GPUs cater to high-end users seeking enhanced graphics capabilities, integrated GPUs serve casual users focused on efficiency and basic performance.</p></p>

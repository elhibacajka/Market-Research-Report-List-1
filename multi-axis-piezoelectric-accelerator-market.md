<p><p>The multi-axis piezoelectric accelerator market encompasses two primary types: linear accelerometers and pendulous accelerometers. Linear accelerometers measure acceleration along a single axis, providing precise data for applications such as structural monitoring and automotive systems. In contrast, pendulous accelerometers utilize a pendulum mechanism to detect changes in motion across multiple axes, making them suitable for applications requiring comprehensive orientation data, such as robotics and aerospace. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials for accurate and responsive performance in various industries.</p></p>

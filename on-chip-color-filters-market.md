<p><strong>The On-Chip Color Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bayer Filter</li><li>RGBE Filter</li><li>RYYB Filter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-chip color filters are essential for image sensors, enhancing color accuracy in photography and videography. The Bayer filter, the most common, uses a 2x2 grid of red, green, and blue pixels to capture color information. The RGBE filter adds a clear pixel, improving light sensitivity and dynamic range. The RYYB filter substitutes yellow for one green pixel, boosting low-light performance. Other types include custom filters for specific applications, catering to varying market needs in the imaging technology industry.</p></p>

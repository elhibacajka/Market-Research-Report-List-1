<p><strong>The Oscilloscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Oscilloscope</li><li>Digital Oscilloscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oscilloscopes market is primarily divided into analog and digital oscilloscopes. Analog oscilloscopes display waveforms as continuous signals on a cathode ray tube, providing real-time visualization of electrical signals. In contrast, digital oscilloscopes capture signals and convert them into digital data for analysis, offering advanced features like storage, processing, and measurement capabilities. Digital oscilloscopes are increasingly favored for their versatility and precision, while analog models are appreciated for their simplicity and ease of use in basic applications.</p></p>

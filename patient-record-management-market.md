<p><p>Patient Record Management (PRM) systems are categorized into two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise solutions are installed locally on healthcare facilities' servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based systems store data on remote servers, providing scalability, accessibility, and reduced IT costs. They enable real-time updates and collaboration, making them ideal for organizations seeking flexibility and lower operational burdens in managing patient records.</p></p>

<p><p>The Polypropylene (PP) filament market comprises two main types: multifilament and monofilament. Polypropylene multifilament consists of multiple intertwined fibers, offering flexibility and strength, making it suitable for textiles, fishing nets, and industrial applications. In contrast, polypropylene monofilament consists of a single, solid fiber, providing durability and versatility in applications like ropes, brushes, and medical products. Both types benefit from polypropylene's lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and cost-effective properties, driving demand across various industries.</p></p>

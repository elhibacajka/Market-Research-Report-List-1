<p><ul><li>Conduction Type</li><li>Convection Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable dry herb vaporizer market is primarily segmented into two types: conduction and convection. Conduction vaporizers heat herbs through direct contact with a heated surface, providing quick results but potentially uneven heating. In contrast, convection vaporizers circulate hot air around the herbs, ensuring uniform heating and enhancing flavor and efficiency. Both types cater to different user preferences, with conduction offering convenience and convection focusing on flavor preservation, influencing consumer choices in the growing vaporizer market.</p></p>

<p><p>The powder metallurgy equipment market consists of several types, each serving distinct functions. Pulverizing equipment is used to grind and refine metal powders for uniformity. Suppression equipment helps in controlling environmental factors during powder processing. Sintering equipment applies heat to consolidate and strengthen powders into solid forms. Degreasing sintering equipment removes contaminants from metal powders before the sintering process, ensuring high-quality end products. Together, these equipment types are essential for efficient powder metallurgy manufacturing.</p></p>

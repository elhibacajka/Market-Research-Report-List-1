<p><p>SFP optical transceiver modules are critical components in optical networks, facilitating data transmission over fiber optics. The "Single-fiber Bidirectional" type allows simultaneous data transmission and reception over a single fiber strand, enhancing efficiency and reducing infrastructure requirements. In contrast, "Dual-fiber Bidirectional" modules use two separate fibers, one dedicated to sending data and the other for receiving, enabling higher bandwidth and improved signal integrity. Both types are essential for various applications, balancing performance and cost in network design.</p></p>

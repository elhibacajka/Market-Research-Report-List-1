<p><p>The Silicone Pressure Sensitive Adhesives (PSA) market is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based and solventless adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain volatile organic compounds that provide strong bonding and flexibility, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, solventless adhesives are developed without solvents, resulting in more environmentally friendly options with lower emissions. They offer excellent performance in terms of adhesion and durability, appealing to industries focused on sustainability while maintaining high-quality bonding solutions.</p></p>

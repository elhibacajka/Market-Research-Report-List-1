<p><p>The Tardive Dyskinesia market encompasses various treatments categorized by their pharmacological actions. Dopamine-depleting medications aim to reduce excess dopamine activity linked to symptoms. GABA receptor agonist medications enhance GABA neurotransmission to counteract dyskinetic movements. Anticholinergic medications work by blocking acetylcholine receptors, which can alleviate some motor symptoms. Each medication type targets different mechanisms in the nervous system, offering distinct therapeutic options for managing Tardive Dyskinesia and improving patient quality of life.</p></p>

<p><p>The torsional damper market comprises three main types: rubber torsional dampers, silicone oil torsional dampers, and composite torsional dampers. Rubber torsional dampers utilize elastic properties of rubber to absorb vibrations and reduce noise in rotating machinery. Silicone oil torsional dampers employ viscous fluids for effective energy dissipation, particularly at varying temperatures. Composite torsional dampers leverage advanced materials for enhanced durability and lightweight characteristics, making them suitable for high-performance applications in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>

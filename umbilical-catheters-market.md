<p><p>The umbilical catheters market is categorized into single-lumen, dual-lumen, and triple-lumen types, each designed for specific clinical needs in neonatal care. Single-lumen catheters allow for basic intravenous access, while dual-lumen catheters provide simultaneous access for fluid administration and blood sampling. Triple-lumen catheters offer enhanced versatility, enabling multiple concurrent therapies, such as medication delivery, nutrition, and blood draws. The choice of catheter type depends on the infant's clinical condition and the complexity of care required.</p></p>
